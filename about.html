ABOUT US

Red Panda is a family run Chinese restaurant and takeway based in Callington, Cornwall in the local pannier market. We've been open for 13 years now meaning we're experienced in delivering you the highest quality food and service that we possibly can. Our restaurant & takeaway is open from 5pm-11pm 6 days a week from Tuesdays to Sundays and we also run an all you can eat buffet on Sundays, Tuesdays, Wednesdays & Thursdays. We are open on all bank holidays including bank holiday mondays however unfortunately we're not open on standard mondays. Red Panda and it's staff enjoy meeting and greeting their customers and we take pride in our friendly service making us perfect for those special occasions.

Our restraunt is part of the new generation of oriental and Thai cuisine and we're sure you'll be fully satisfied with our food. Our restaurant also includes a fully licensed bar with a variety of different wines, alcholic beverages and soft drinks.
